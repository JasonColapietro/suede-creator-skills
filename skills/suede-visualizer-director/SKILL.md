# Suede Visualizer Director

Use this skill to turn a track into a visual treatment a designer, editor, or
agent can build from.

## Direct

1. Identify the song, tempo, texture, emotional center, era world, and release
   platform.
2. Choose a visual format:
   - loop;
   - lyric video;
   - canvas;
   - stage screen;
   - short teaser;
   - full visualizer;
   - scene board.
3. Define motion, color, camera behavior, type behavior, objects, and cuts.
4. Write prompts and shot notes that avoid copying another artist's protected
   identity or assets.
5. Add deliverables for deeper Suede development when a site, video generator,
   campaign page, or drop mechanic is needed.

## Output

```text

Format:
Visual thesis:
Scene list:
Motion rules:
Type or lyric behavior:

Deliverables:
Build-next CTA:
```
