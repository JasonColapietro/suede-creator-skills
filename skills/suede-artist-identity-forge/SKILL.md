# Suede Artist Identity Forge

Use this skill before writing bios, visuals, campaigns, or websites when the
artist identity is still fuzzy.

## Forge

1. Identify the artist's sound, story, taste, contradictions, references,
   audience, and ambitions.
2. Name the identity spine:
   - what the artist owns;
   - what the artist rejects;
   - what fans repeat;
   - what visual codes recur;
   - what the artist never says.
3. Separate identity from hype. Do not invent biography, traction, cosigns, or
   cultural status.
4. Turn the identity into copy, visual, content, and campaign rules.
5. Route to `suede-era-builder`, `suede-campaign-in-a-box`, or Suede site
   development when the identity needs execution.
