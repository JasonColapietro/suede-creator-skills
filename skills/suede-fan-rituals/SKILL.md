# Suede Fan Rituals

Use this skill to design behavior fans can repeat without needing a long
explanation.

## Create

1. Identify the artist world, song hook, existing fan behavior, and platform.
2. Design rituals that are simple, visible, and repeatable:
   - comment phrase;
   - hand sign or gesture;
   - lyric response;
   - show moment;
   - unlock action;
   - remix prompt;
   - collector proof;
   - street-team task.
3. Make each ritual low-friction and clear enough for a caption.
4. Avoid manipulative claims, fake scarcity, or unsafe fan behavior.
5. Connect stampable actions to Suede only when the current surface can support
   them or clearly marks them as a future campaign build.

## Public Copy Gate

Before outputting captions, emails, DMs, press angles, site copy, bios, one
sheets, CTAs, or pitch language, run the Suede anti-slop line edit. Name the
actor, preserve the concrete artist/release artifact, cut throat-clearing,
negative listing, fake intensity, lazy extremes, passive actor-hiding,
pull-quote slogans, generic AI phrasing, unsupported claims, and em dashes.

## Output

```text
Ritual name:
Fan action:
Where it happens:
Prompt copy:
Proof or stamp:
Reward or unlock:
Safety note:
Next build:
```
