What it covers

  • Standalone conversion email, landing page copy, CTAs, microcopy, and button labels.
  • Company brief inputs for product, audience, voice, terms, proof, allowed claims, forbidden claims, and primary CTA.
  • Twelve headline formulas and five persuasion frameworks matched to surface and reader temperature.
  • A/B variants for headlines, CTAs, email subjects, and ad copy.
  • Email, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, and Instagram structures with platform-specific rules.
  • An anti-slop line-edit gate, word substitution list, readability checks, and copy score.

When to use it

  • Use it for standalone conversion email, landing page copy, CTAs, microcopy, or button labels.
  • Use it when page copy, GitHub docs, email, or social posts need specific, proof-backed language.
  • Use it when a hero headline, primary CTA, email subject, or ad needs labeled variants.
  • Use it when draft copy needs anti-slop review and scoring before handoff.
  • Not for: full writing stack with SEO and AI Engine Optimization (use Johnny Suede Write).
  • Not for: multi-email campaign arcs or campaign performance reporting (use private Suede Labs companion suede-growth, not in this pack).
  • Not for: post-production AI-pattern removal from already-written copy (use private Suede Labs companion suede-deslop, not in this pack).
