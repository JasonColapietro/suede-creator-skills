Suede SEO Audit.

Suede SEO Audit runs a nine-lane SEO, AEO, and AI EO audit for public pages. Use it when the audit itself is the deliverable: source-truth checks, metadata, schema, AI citation readiness, exact rewrites, lane grades, and a ship gate.

What it covers

  • Live source-truth checks for status code, redirects, robots, canonical URL, sitemap presence, metadata, JSON-LD, and primary H1.
  • Nine audit lanes: technical access, search and answer intent, metadata, structure, schema, AI EO, copy quality, E-E-A-T, and topic clusters.
  • Optional keyword research with primary, secondary, and LSI classification, competitor gaps, SERP feature targets, and a content brief.
  • Ranked findings with severity, location, issue, exact fix, suggested copy or code, and verification steps.
  • Mechanical lane grades, weighted overall scoring, hard caps, and a ship, ship-with-caveats, or hold gate.

When to use it

  • Audit SEO, AEO, and AI EO before a public launch.
  • Find why a page is not ranking or is not cited by AI answers.
  • Validate schema, metadata, crawler access, and AI citation signals.
  • Run keyword or competitor gap research as part of the audit.
  • Not for: a fast A-F promotion-readiness verdict (use Suede Visibility Grader).
  • Not for: conversion and CRO rewrites (use Suede Site Alchemy).
  • Not for: writing fresh copy (use Suede Copy).

Best prompt

Use $suede-seo-audit to audit https://example.com/pricing for SEO, AEO, and AI EO. Verify live source truth, check metadata and schema, run all active lanes, write exact fixes, score each lane, and set the ship gate.
